Duels of the Ancient - A desktop card game influenced by DotA, based on Yu-Gi-Oh!.

1
Title: Receiving cards
Description: Player receives cards which can be heroes or items.All cards should be able to have descriptions.
The 'cards' are abstract and are in the form of text.No graphics planned for Iteration #1.


2
Title: Usability of the cards
Description: Every card, when played, should be able to utilize it's properties such as attack,heal,etc.
For example, a hero can perform a normal attack on the enemy player's hero.

3
Title: Alternative game endings
Description: Victory/defeat outcome based on >who is going to lose his HP points first<
